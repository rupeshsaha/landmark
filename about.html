Who We Are

Landmark Realty specializes in acquiring and managing traditional multifamily housing & furnished corporate apartments. With over 50 years of success, we have expanded to more than 7,600 units across a portfolio of over 70 properties nationwide. Landmark's business began in 1968 with a 4-unit building in San Francisco's historic Mission District, where Landmark's headquarters are located today. Over the last five decades, Landmark has expanded its operations into Northern California, New Mexico, Kansas, Missouri, and Illinois.

Our success is based on our core values. We embrace and take pride in offering an inclusive environment. We believe in treating all people fairly and with respect. Above all else, we remain dedicated to providing our residents with quality housing and customer service at competitive prices.

CULTURE

Mission

To provide exceptional multifamily housing solutions that prioritize quality, fairness, and value, enriching the lives of our residents and fostering vibrant communities.

Vision

To be the leading multifamily housing company recognized for our unwavering commitment to quality living spaces, fair and inclusive practices, and exceptional value, setting the industry standard and positively impacting the lives of our residents and communities.

CORE VALUE

Our team is dedicated to providing outstanding value and strongly believes in treating all people fairly, respectfully, and with integrity. At Landmark, we believe in the power of inclusivity and diversity and their capacity to enrich our communities. We take pride in offering an inclusive environment where your contributions are appreciated and valued.

GUIDING PRINCIPLES

Quality

We are dedicated to delivering the highest quality standards in every aspect of our multifamily housing developments. We prioritize attention to detail, craftsmanship, and quality materials to ensure our residents enjoy comfortable, safe, and aesthetically appealing living spaces. Our commitment to quality extends to our customer service, maintenance, and ongoing property management.

Fairness

We strongly believe in treating all stakeholders with respect, fairness, and integrity. We foster an inclusive and diverse environment, ensuring equal opportunities and promoting transparency and open communication. We strive to create fair and equitable policies, procedures, and pricing structures, ensuring that our residents, employees, and business partners are treated fairly in all interactions.

Value

We are dedicated to providing outstanding value to our residents by offering multifamily housing options that meet their needs and exceed their expectations at competitive prices. We continuously seek innovative ways to optimize efficiency and cost-effectiveness throughout our operations, without compromising quality or fairness. We aim to deliver exceptional value for every dollar, emphasizing affordability and a positive living experience.
